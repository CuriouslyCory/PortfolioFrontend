assessment 3d Printing and Design

I took board drafting and AutoCAD classes in high school and always wished I could take my designs from the virtual space into real world. Christmas 2016 my dream became reality when I was gifted a 3d printer. I'll print hobby parts and models for myself and friends, design random components, and have even created a child saftey box for my electrical outlets. One day I'd like to design, print, and build a robot.

assessment Hiking, Backpacking, and Camping

I grew up in the Pacific Northwest and have always had a love for the outdoors. My dad taught me the local edible plantlife and I did a lot of camping with him and the boyscounts. These days my family and I will take any opportunity to get into the mountains and enjoy the pines, or take a stroll through the low desert when snow packs the rim roads.
